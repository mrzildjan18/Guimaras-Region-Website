Activities in Guimaras

Learn more about Guimaras Society

Swim in Buho Ramirez Cave
Guimaras Province

It's worth the trip to Buho Ramirez Cave just to see the rock formations and the hundreds of fruit bats that call it home.

Spend tour hiking breaks at Ambakan Falls
Guimaras Province

From Jordan Wharf, you can see all the beauty of Ambakan Falls by taking a short drive of about three kilometers.

Chow down on fresh seafood goodness
Guimaras Province

There appears to be an abundance of shrimp, squid, fish, seaweed, and other seafaring delicacies.

Spend quality time with your significant other in the most romantic spots
Guimaras Province

The Guimaras wind farm, which was built in the second part of 2014, is a sight to behold.

Take a break from the tropical landscapes to visit a wind farm
Guimaras Province

Erected during the latter half of 2014, the wind farm at Guimaras is definitely a thing of beauty.

Visit the Trappist Monastery and gift shop
Guimaras Province

Visitors to the Trappist Monastery are invited to relax and reflect with quiet pursuits like prayer and meditation.

Study the different fish species at SEAFDEC
Guimaras Province

The Southeast Asian Fisheries Development Center is a research center where they breed and study different fish species.

Snorkel in Ave Maria Islet
Guimaras Province

Have you made up your mind about what to do in Guimaras, or do you still need some guidance? The Ave Maria Islet is a must-see on every multi-island excursion.

Visit Roca Encantada, an incredible heritage house
Guimaras Province

One of the most famous landmarks of Guimaras is Roca Encantada. It is known to be a heritage house that dates all the way back to 1910.
