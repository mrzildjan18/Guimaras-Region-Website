Take a break from the tropical landscapes to visit a wind farm

Guimaras Province JC Rahman A. Nava 8 Likes

Guimaras is located in Western Visayas Region in the Philippines, a sandwich island province between the islands of Panay and Negros. The capital municipality is Jordan and people speak of Hiligaynon, Kinaray-a, Ati, Filipino and English languages. There are just around 200,000+ residents in the province. The island province of Guimaras is one of the top tourist destinations in the country as well as its nearby islands. If you are planning to visit here so soon, below are the best tourist spots in Guimaras to make your trip worth it.

Erected during the latter half of 2014, the wind farm at Guimaras is definitely a thing of beauty. Here you’re going to see miles and miles of San Lorenzo’s lush countryside with gigantic wind turbines scattered across the horizon. It’s a view quite different from the tropical paradise that exists several minutes away.
